Data Analyst (Sample)

Responsibilities
- Build and maintain SQL queries and dashboards to track key business metrics.
- Clean, normalize, and validate data from multiple sources; document data quality issues.
- Perform ad-hoc analysis and communicate insights to stakeholders.
- Partner with product/ops teams to define KPIs and improve reporting workflows.
- Support experimentation and cohort analysis (funnels, retention, segmentation).

Requirements
- Strong SQL and data modeling fundamentals.
- Proficiency in Python for analysis/automation.
- Experience with BI tools and data visualization.
- Familiarity with statistics (hypothesis testing) and metrics design.
- Excellent communication and ability to translate data into decisions.
